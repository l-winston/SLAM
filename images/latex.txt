StateUpdateEquation
\hat{x}_{n, n} = \hat{x}_{n, n-1} + K_n (z_n - \hat{x}_{n, n-1})

StateExtrapolationEquation1
\hat{x}_{n+1, n} = \hat{x}_{n, n} + \Delta t \hat{ \dot{x} }_{n, n}

StateExtrapolationEquation2
\hat{\dot{x}}_{n+1, n} = \hat{\dot{x}}_{n, n}

KalmanGainEquation
K_n = \frac{p_{n, n-1}}{p_{n, n-1} + r_n}

CovarianceUpdateEquation
p_{n, n} = (1-K_n)p_{n, n-1}

CovarianceExtrapolationEquation
p_{n+1, n} = p_{n, n}
